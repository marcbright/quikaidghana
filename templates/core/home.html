    How it works

    From alert to action in three beats.

    A calm flow for stressed moments: capture, verify, and broadcast — without losing the human story in the data.

    1. 1

      Report or explore

      Submit an issue with location and category, or scan the dashboard and map for what’s unfolding near you.

    2. 2

      Operators triage fast

      Staff update statuses, merge duplicates, and prioritise life-safety items through Django admin built for speed.

    3. 3

      Community stays informed

      Citizens see verified signals, SOS pathways, and assistant guidance — optimised for mobile networks.
