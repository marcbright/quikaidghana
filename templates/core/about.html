About QuickAid Ghana

A modern emergency, utility, and community reporting layer for Ghana — built for clarity under pressure.

We focus on dumsor and utilities, traffic and fuel queues, water shortages, hospitals, SOS numbers, and live-style alerts citizens can trust. The product direction is “if a major tech company built civic infrastructure for Ghana”.

This build is hackathon-ready: SQLite or PostgreSQL, Bootstrap 5, and map-friendly data models.

Principles

  • Fast paths for reporting and discovery
  • Mobile-first, readable in sunlight and at night
  • Admin-grade data quality for demos
  • Maps and chat as first-class surfaces
